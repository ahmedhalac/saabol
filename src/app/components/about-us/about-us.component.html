About Us

Where Caring Meets Compassion

With a deep sense of compassion and unwavering faith, we offer a safe and nurturing environment where residents can find comfort, community and a path to holistic well-being.

Fostering Independence and Compassion for Senior and Disabled Veterans

Welcome to SAABOL, where our unwavering dedication is to enhance the independence, dignity, and well-being of our senior and disabled veteran community.

We are a compassionate ministry nonprofit organization committed to providing accessible, barrier-free senior assisted living options and a comprehensive array of support services.

Our objective is to empower the heroes and citizens who have selflessly served our nation to lead fulfilling lives, surrounded by care, respect, and spiritual guidance.

Holistic Care

We understand that every resident is unique and deserves individualized care.

Our compassionate caregivers create customized care plans that encompass physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being, ensuring that residents receive the attention they deserve.

Spiritual Nourishment

As a ministry nonprofit, we place a strong emphasis on spiritual well-being.

We offer spiritual guidance, religious services, and opportunities for residents to connect with their faith community. We believe in nurturing the soul alongside the body.

Community Living

We foster a sense of community where residents can build meaningful relationships with their fellow residents and caregivers.

Our common areas and engaging activities encourage socialization and a feeling of belonging.

Nutritious Meals

Good nutrition is essential for health and well-being.

Our talented culinary team prepares well-balanced, delicious meals that cater to individual dietary needs and preferences.

Residents can savor home-cooked meals in a warm and communal setting.

24/7 Support

Our dedicated staff is available round-the-clock to provide assistance and support to our residents.

Whether it's help with daily activities or a reassuring presence during the night, we're here to ensure our residents' safety and comfort.

Safe and Reliable Transportation

We offer scheduled transportation services to medical appointments, therapy sessions, and other healthcare needs.

Our well-maintained vehicles and trained drivers provide a comfortable and secure transportation experience.

Compassionate Support

Our drivers are not just professionals but also compassionate individuals who understand the unique needs of our residents.

They provide assistance and reassurance throughout the transportation process, ensuring a stress-free experience.

Wheelchair Accessibility

Our vehicles are equipped to accommodate residents with mobility challenges, including those requiring wheelchair-accessible transportation.

We prioritize accessibility and comfort for all our residents.

Coordinated Care

Our team works closely with residents and their families to coordinate transportation, ensuring timely and hassle-free access to healthcare services.

We're here to provide the necessary support during medical visits.

At SAABOL, our mission is driven by a profound sense of gratitude and respect for the courageous men and women who have dedicated their lives to defending our freedom. We are guided by the following core principles:

Independence

We believe in the inherent right of every individual, regardless of age or physical abilities, to live life on their own terms. Our goal is to create environments where senior and disabled veterans can maintain their independence and continue to enjoy life to the fullest.

Compassion

Our compassionate team is committed to providing personalized care and support that goes beyond physical needs. We recognize the importance of nurturing the spirit and providing spiritual guidance to those who seek it.

Community

We foster a sense of belonging and camaraderie within our communities, offering residents the opportunity to build meaningful connections and find solace in the company of their peers.
